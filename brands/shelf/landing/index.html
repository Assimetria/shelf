Features

Everything your library needs

From quick saves to deep research sessions — Shelf keeps every piece of content organized and at your fingertips.

One-click saves

Install the browser extension and save any page in under a second. Shelf automatically extracts the title, description, and reading time.

Smart collections

Group content into collections that update automatically based on your rules. Tag once — the right content flows to the right place.

Full-text search

Search across everything you've ever saved — titles, content, notes, and highlights. Find any article in milliseconds.

Highlights & notes

Mark up any saved page with highlights and personal notes. Build a second brain from the content that matters most to you.

Team sharing

Share collections with teammates. Build a shared research library that everyone can contribute to and discover from.

AI-powered discovery

Shelf learns what you read and surfaces forgotten gems and related content at the perfect moment. Your library gets smarter over time.

How it works

From save to insight in 3 steps

No more lost tabs, forgotten links, or cluttered bookmarks. Shelf makes knowledge capture effortless.

1

Save anything

Use the browser extension, mobile share sheet, or paste any URL directly. Shelf captures the full content offline.

2

Organize your way

Add tags, create collections, and write notes as you go. Smart filters do the heavy lifting automatically.

3

Find it when you need it

Search your entire library in milliseconds. AI surfaces the right content at the right time — without you asking.
