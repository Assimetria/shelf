Features

Everything you need to
organise your content

From collecting to publishing, Shelf handles every step of your content workflow with elegance and power.

Collect anything

Save articles, videos, links, files, and notes from anywhere. Use the browser extension, email forwarding, or drag-and-drop upload to capture content instantly.

Smart collections

Organise content into curated collections with custom layouts, tags, and smart filters. Build the perfect shelf for every project, topic, or team.

Publish beautifully

Share your shelf with a stunning public page. Custom themes, layouts, and your own domain — no design skills needed.

Built-in analytics

Understand which content resonates. Track views, clicks, engagement, and shares across all your collections.

Shareable links

Generate instant share links for any collection or item. Control access with password protection, expiry dates, and view permissions.

Team collaboration

Invite your team to contribute, comment, and curate together. Assign roles and build a shared knowledge base that grows with your organisation.

How it works

Three steps to your
perfect content home

Get started in minutes. No complex setup, no steep learning curve.

1

Collect

2

Curate

Organise into collections, add tags, write notes, and arrange layouts. Make your content feel intentional, not chaotic.

3

Share

Publish to the world or share privately with your team. Beautiful presentation that reflects the quality of your content.
