Features

Everything in one place, nothing gets lost

Shelf turns your digital chaos into a curated, searchable library you can actually use.

Save anything

Capture links, PDFs, images, notes, tweets, YouTube videos, and code snippets with one click. Works with your browser extension or share sheet.

Full-text search

Search across titles, URLs, notes, and even the full content of saved pages. Find anything in milliseconds, no matter how big your library grows.

Smart collections

Organize items into collections with custom tags and filters. Create nested collections for complex projects. Share collections with your team.

Team sharing

Share individual items or entire collections. Set view, comment, or edit permissions. Keep your team aligned without spamming Slack links.

AI summaries

Get automatic AI-generated summaries for every saved article or document. Extract key insights without reading the full content every time.

Web clipper

Browser extension for Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Clip full pages, articles, or just selections. Auto-captures metadata, tags, and source URL.

Organization

Never lose track of what matters

Shelf auto-organizes your content with smart tags and surfacing. Spend less time filing, more time finding.

  • Auto-tagging based on content type and URL domain
  • Duplicate detection — no more double-saves
  • Archive old items automatically after inactivity
  • Reading progress tracking for long-form content
  • Bulk operations for tagging, moving, and deleting
